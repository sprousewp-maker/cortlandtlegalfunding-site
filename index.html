FAQ

What clients usually ask

What is legal funding?

Legal funding is a cash advance made against a pending legal claim. Terms vary, so clients should review the contract carefully and consult counsel.

Do I make monthly payments?

Typically, no monthly payments are required. Repayment is generally tied to the outcome of the case, subject to the terms of the funding agreement.

Do you work directly with my attorney?

Yes. Funding decisions usually depend on case documentation and attorney cooperation.

How fast can I get funded?

Timing depends on how quickly the case can be reviewed and documents completed, but the process is designed to move quickly.
